ItinenaryAtlas

Vacation Trip Planner

My Role: UI/UX Designer

Project Duration: 3 months

Problem Statement

Making an itinerary when travel ing can get hectic and when traveling to a new place, it can be an issue to find stuff to do.

Solution

Make a website where people can put their input for traveling at a certain location. People can post their itineraries for locations and other people can work off that. Overall, make the traveling experience much easier and less stressful.
